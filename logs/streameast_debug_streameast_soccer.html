Stream quality depends on the original source. We surface all available quality options so you can pick the best fit for your connection.

Can I cast streams to my TV?

Do you have mobile apps?

StreamEast is fully mobile-optimized and supports PWA features — add the site to your home screen for an app-like experience.

Is it legal to stream NFL games online?

Official NFL games are licensed to broadcasters and streaming services in many regions. StreamEast indexes publicly available feeds; for licensed access, use official platforms (NFL+, network partners, etc.) and follow local regulations.

Can I watch all NFL games with one service?

No single service carries every NFL game worldwide — rights are split between networks and platforms. Local blackout rules may also apply.

What internet speed do I need for NFL streaming?

For HD streaming we recommend 5–10 Mbps. For 4K streams aim for 25 Mbps or higher. A wired connection or strong Wi-Fi improves stability.

What's the difference between NFL+ and NFL RedZone?

NFL+ provides live local games and on-demand replays. NFL RedZone shows live highlight coverage focused on scoring plays across multiple games.
